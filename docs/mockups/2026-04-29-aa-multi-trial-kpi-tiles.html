Recommendation

Variant B across all three surfaces (Section 1 headline, Section 1 KPI tiles, Section 3 Competitive Position, Playbook Scorecard). Rationale:

  • Visual hierarchy: bold percent reads first, +/- reads second; matches existing typographic emphasis logic.
  • Information density: lands the +/- without crowding the headline.
  • Consistency: same treatment across PDF and Playbook reduces visual reconciliation cost for the customer.

Variant A is busier on the headline display. Variant C is the most conservative but loses the at-a-glance "the answer is roughly 40 percent and we are 95 percent confident in that" read.

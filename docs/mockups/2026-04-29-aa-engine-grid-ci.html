Recommendation

Apply Variant B verbatim. The engine-grid is a tighter container than the Section 01 KPI strip, so the CI line uses 11px (vs 12px on KPI tiles) but the same slate-700 color, 700 weight, tabular-nums alignment.

Note the .engine-stat line gains a parenthetical share percent (e.g., 12 / 30 trials cited (40%)). Today's stat line reads 4 / 10 queries cited; under N=3 trials, the meaningful unit is now trials, not queries. The proposed line surfaces the trial count + parenthetical percent so the percent on the bar (40 percent visual) and the percent in copy match. Alternatively, we could keep 4 / 10 queries cited (queries-cited == queries with majority-of-trials cited per Q5 back-compat) and just add the CI line beneath; that is a smaller diff but loses the trial-level granularity.

What's in each monthly report

  1. Overall citation share + 95% confidence interval. Aggregated across all 12 trials.
  2. Per-engine breakdown. ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity - which engines cite you, which do not.
  3. Competitor landscape. Every domain that co-appeared in this month's responses, ranked by mention count.

How it differs from the full audit

Citation Monitor watches one prompt in 12 LLM calls per month - cheap, fast, and trend-focused. The SXO Audit at /start runs 60 measurements per brand (5 buyer prompts × 4 engines × 3 trials), inspects your schema, and ships a Playbook with copy-paste remediations. Use Citation Monitor to track movement; use SXO Audit to fix root causes.

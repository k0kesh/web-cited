01 - Definition

What is SXO?

Search Experience Optimization is the discipline of earning visibility across every surface where buyers now look for answers - traditional search engines, answer engines, and generative models.

SEO gets you ranked. AEO gets you answered. GEO gets you cited by AI. SXO makes sure all three happen at once, with messaging that survives when your paragraph is rewritten by a language model.

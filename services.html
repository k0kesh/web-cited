02 - Flagship · $5,000

SXO Audit

ONE-TIME · 10 BUSINESS DAYS · ASYNC

The flagship. A comprehensive, actionable, sitewide audit of how humans, search engines, and AI models find, quote, and cite your business - paired with a ticket-ready fix backlog and a per-page schema pack.

What it covers

A · SEO

Classic Search

Crawlability, indexation, Core Web Vitals, internal linking, keyword coverage, title/meta hygiene, schema markup, and E-E-A-T signals.

B · AEO

Answer Engines

Featured snippet eligibility, People-Also-Ask coverage, structured Q&A content, voice-search readiness, and Google AI Overview presence.

C · GEO

Generative Engines

Citation share across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Claude for your priority prompts. Source-page analysis so you know why you were cited (or not).

D · Discoverability

Content & Surfaces

Reddit, YouTube, Wikipedia, Google Knowledge Graph, Wikidata, and Google Business Profile: the off-site pages and records that LLMs and answer engines quote when they write their answers.

What you receive

  1. The SXO ReportOne comprehensive, actionable, sitewide report covering SEO, AEO, GEO, entity presence, and off-site discoverability - structured so a product marketer, an SEO lead, and an engineer can each act from their own section.
  2. Competitive citation matrixPrompt-by-prompt, engine-by-engine map of where your competitors are being cited and you are not. CSV export included.
  3. Prioritized, ticket-ready fix backlogEvery recommendation tagged with owner, effort estimate, expected impact, and acceptance criteria - paste-ready for Jira, Linear, or Asana.
  4. Per-page JSON-LD schema packPaste-ready <script type="application/ld+json"> blocks tailored to each priority page's content type - pre-validated against Schema.org and Google's Rich Results Test.
  5. Async Loom walkthroughA tailored pre-recorded walkthrough of the report, delivered with the deliverables. Watchable on your own schedule, shareable with your team.
  6. 30-day async Q&AEmail questions answered for a month after delivery, so the report actually gets implemented.

Status: drafting and legal review Expected publication: 2026 Q2

What governs your engagement today

If you have submitted the intake form at /start, the scope email Web Cited sent (or will send) you is the operative engagement contract. It states the brand or brands being audited, the URLs and buyer-question prompts in scope, the tier price, the turnaround commitment, and the deliverables. Your payment of the corresponding invoice constitutes acceptance of that scope.

What this page will cover when published

The full Terms of Service will cover, among other things:

  • Acceptance, eligibility, and modifications

  • Services description and tier scope
  • Intake, scope confirmation, fees, payment, refunds, and turnaround commitments
  • Customer responsibilities and the license to Customer Content
  • Deliverables and an audit-accuracy disclaimer
  • Intellectual property ownership and the license granted with deliverables
  • Confidentiality
  • Warranties and disclaimers (including AS-IS service)
  • Limitation of liability and a liability cap
  • Indemnification, balanced both ways
  • Term and termination, with surviving sections
  • Governing law (California) and venue (Orange County, California)
  • Binding arbitration with a class action waiver and a small claims carveout
  • Privacy policy incorporation and data security commitments
  • Service availability disclaimer (no SLA-level uptime guarantee on the Site)
  • Export control and sanctions compliance
  • Intellectual property infringement claim process
  • Independent contractor relationship (no fiduciary, no agency, no partnership)
  • Electronic communications consent and e-signature validity
  • Stripe relationship and payment processor disclaimer
  • General provisions: entire agreement, severability, no waiver, assignment, force majeure, notices

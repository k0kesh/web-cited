Roto-Rooter vs Mr. Rooter in AI search.

The finding in one paragraph

Service-area businesses (plumbing, HVAC, electrical) compete for the moment a customer types an emergency search into Google or, increasingly, an AI assistant. We tested 5 standardized buyer questions a homeowner or facility manager might plausibly ask. Each question ran 3 times per engine across 4 engines. The result, per-engine breakdown, and per-prompt comparison are below.

The 5 standardized buyer prompts

  1. Who is the best national plumbing service for emergency repairs in the United States?
  2. What national plumbing franchise has the most US coverage and 24-hour availability?
  3. Which plumbing service provides the fastest emergency response for residential customers?
  4. What are the top national plumbing service companies for commercial buildings?
  5. Who should a homeowner call for an emergency clogged drain or backed-up sewer line?
Known limitations. Substring matching can miss paraphrased mentions. LLM responses vary; N=3 reduces but does not eliminate variance. Same domain-based methodology as the full Web Cited audit.

FAQ

Common questions.

If your question isn't here, email hello@web-cited.com and we'll respond inside one business day.

Is the $5,000 fee truly fixed?

Yes. Fixed scope, fixed price. The only variable is sites with more than one subdomain or more than five buyer personas — we'll flag scope on the intake call before any invoice goes out.

How is this different from a traditional SEO audit?

Traditional SEO audits focus on google.com. We test Google, Bing, Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Claude — then score citation share per prompt. The SEO work is included; it's just a subset of the surface we cover.

What is included in the $5,000?

A 60–90 page SXO report, a competitive citation matrix, a prioritized fix backlog, a JSON-LD schema pack, a 60-minute walkthrough call, and 30 days of async follow-up questions. No additional invoices.

Can you work under a DPA / NDA / MSA?

Yes. We sign standard enterprise DPAs and NDAs, and we can work under a buyer-paper MSA if one is required. We do not bill for legal review.

Do you offer ongoing retainers or subscriptions?

No. Web Cited sells exactly one thing — a one-time SXO Audit. We do not offer managed services, content production, link building, or tracking subscriptions. If you need those, we can recommend partners.

Who runs the audit?

A senior practitioner — not a junior account handler. See About for our positioning and the kinds of engagements we take on.
